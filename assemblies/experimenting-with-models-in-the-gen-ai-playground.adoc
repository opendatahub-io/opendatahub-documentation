= Experimenting with models in the gen AI playground

Use the generative AI (gen AI) playground feature in {productname-short} to evaluate, test, and interact with foundation and custom models in your project. You can test prompt engineering with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and validate model behavior before using the model in an application.
