= Migrating your accelerator profile

[role='_abstract']
To take advantage of the hardware profiles feature, you must first migrate your accelerator profile. You can migrate your accelerator profile using the [productname-long] web console.

.Prerequisites
. You have logged in to {productname-short} as a user with {productname-short} administrator privileges.
. You have an existing Accelerator profile.
 
.Procedure
. From the {productname-short} dashboard, click *Settings* -> *Environment setup* -> *Hardware profiles*.
+
The *Accelerator profiles* page opens, displaying existing accelerator profiles.
. Click the action menu (*&#8942;*) beside the accelerator profile that you want to migrate and click *Migrate*.
+
The *Migrate accelerator profile* dialog opens.
. Click *Migrate*.

.Verification
* Your accelerator profile is displayed on the *Hardware profiles* page, and not on the *Accelerator profiles* page.
